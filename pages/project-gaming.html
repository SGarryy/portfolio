User Personas Discovered
⚔️ Hardcore Grinders
High Session Hours · High Milestones

Power users with the highest playtime and achievement rates. Strategy: allocate high-priority server bandwidth and premium progression rewards to retain this VIP segment.

🎉 Weekend Casuals
Low Frequency · Moderate Sessions

Irregular players who engage in longer sessions when they do log in. Strategy: trigger "Weekend Warrior" event notifications to maximize their session windows.

🤝 Social Butterflies
High Frequency · Short Sessions

Players who check in often but briefly — driven by social or competitive features. Strategy: prioritize low-latency multiplayer infrastructure for this segment.

⚠️ At-Risk / Idle
Low Engagement Across All Metrics

Users showing disengagement across every dimension. Strategy: deploy automated re-engagement campaigns before full churn occurs.

Pipeline & Methodology
01
Feature Engineering & Schema Standardization

Renamed raw columns: PlayTimeHours→session_duration_hr, SessionsPerWeek→weekly_frequency, AchievementsUnlocked→milestones_reached.

02
Data Validation — 40,000+ Records

Automated null detection, schema consistency, record count verification. Confirmed 0% null values before any modeling.

03
Z-Score Normalization via StandardScaler

Applied before clustering — prevents any single feature from dominating cluster assignment due to scale differences.

04
Elbow Method → Optimal k=4

Swept k from 2–10, plotted inertia. The elbow at k=4 was statistically clear before fitting the final model.

05
K-Means Clustering & Model Persistence

Fitted K-Means (k=4) on normalized features. Saved model as .pkl to /models/ for full reproducibility.

06
Persona Profiling & Executive Reporting

Each cluster labeled, profiled, and translated into a concrete business recommendation with cluster charts and an executive summary.
