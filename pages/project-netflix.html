Key Findings
🎭
Genre Dominance

Drama holds 36% of the catalog — nearly 1 in 3 titles. Comedy is a distant second at 25%. Combined they cover over 60% of Netflix's library.

📅
Post-2015 Content Explosion

Content additions grew 3× after 2015 — driven by Netflix's original content push. The platform added more titles in 2018–2019 than in all years before 2015 combined.

🌍
US Dominance in Production

The USA accounts for ~35% of all catalog content. India and the UK follow — but non-English originals have grown significantly post-2019.
